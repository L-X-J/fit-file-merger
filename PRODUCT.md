# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who record workouts across multiple devices or segments and need one clean FIT export without sending their activity data to a server. They are usually comfortable with training metrics, but they do not want to think about file internals while merging.

## Product Purpose
FIT File Merger helps users combine multiple activity files into a single valid FIT output with local-only processing, route preview, and enough controls to trust the result before download. Success means the workflow feels fast, dependable, and safe for personal activity data.

## Brand Personality
Calm, capable, precise.

The product should feel like a focused utility: confident without showing off, technical without being intimidating, and polished enough that users trust the exported file.

## Anti-references
- Marketing-style hero layouts that bury the actual tool below the fold.
- Overly playful sports visuals that make the app feel less trustworthy.
- Dark, dashboard-heavy admin styling with noisy gradients and decorative metrics.
- Generic "AI SaaS" surfaces with large empty cards, vague copy, and ornamental motion.

## Design Principles
- Keep the work visible: upload state, merge settings, route preview, and export action should stay on screen together.
- Earn trust through clarity: explain state changes, validate inputs early, and surface errors next to the affected files.
- Stay local-first in tone and layout: privacy should be obvious, not hidden in fine print.
- Make the primary action unmistakable: one clear path from validated files to merged export.
- Prefer steady refinement over spectacle: motion and color should support comprehension, not compete with it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard access, visible focus states, and readable mobile layouts. Keep motion subtle and avoid interactions that depend on hover alone.
